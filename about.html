Hailing from Bergen, Astroturf is a rising pop-rock sensation featuring five young but accomplished musicians hailing from different parts of Norway. Known for their high-energy rock music with soaring choruses, earworm melodies, and guitar solos that harken back to the 80s, this band offers a rock experience that's both authentic and unforgettable. The band's story began with Susanne and Alvin, who fell in love and started writing songs together. Before long, they decided to form a band, which came to fruition in February 2022. Since then, they've recorded four singles and released one, while also performing at four concerts. Moreover, they have an upcoming gig every month this spring. But Astroturf is more than just a band. They're also committed to sustainability, as evidenced by their innovative merch project. Instead of buying new shirts, they asked friends and acquaintances to donate old ones that were then sent for printing. Their ultimate goal is to showcase these eco-friendly tees at the Europavox campus, with all shirts sourced from second-hand stores or friends.
